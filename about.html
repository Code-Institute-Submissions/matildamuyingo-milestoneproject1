My Story

Workout history

Gymnastics

When I was 5 years old I started doing artistic gymnastics in a Swedish club named SOL-Flickorna. When I realised that this was a newfound passion of mine I continued evolving my skills. At around 13 years old I switched to Team Gymnastics and started compeating at a higher level. This lead to a long list of titles, one of witch is gaining a silver medal in the European Championchip 2010.

Crossfit

I started crossfit a few years back after doing Gymnastics for the better part of my life. I found crossfit and fell in love with this type of workout. Since beginning my crossfit-journey I've come a long way and have compeated in a few diferent competitions. As late as 2019 I participated in the Crossfit Open and finished as nr. 25 in all of Sweden.
